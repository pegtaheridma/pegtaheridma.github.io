Meme Mashup


 
A mashup (also mesh, mash up, mash-up, blend, or bootleg) is a creative work, usually in a 
form of a song, created by blending two or more pre-recorded songs, usually by superimposing the 
vocal track of one song seamlessly over the instrumental track of another, increasing the 
tempo and pitch while adding or reducing gaps to make it flow. To the extent that such works 
are "transformative" of original content, in the United States they may find protection from 
copyright claims under the "fair use" doctrine of copyright law.  -- wikipedia
