Covid-19 Purge

Bootstrap Example



For my final project, I chose to work with photoshop to make a short animation. This animation is a tribute to the healthcare workers that they have been working very hard fighting against the virus. For this piece, I illustrated the characters using Procreate in iPad Pro Max and then I used photoshop framing option and created the animation.
