Pegah Taheri

Pegah, is originally from Tehran/Iran. She was born and raised in Iran and moved to Turkey when she was 18. She grew her passion in performing art in Ballet industry. She was a dancer/choreographer in San Francisco Ballet from 2016-2019. Then she joined the design/sale team in luxury fashion industry, Louis Vuitton. She currently is a client expert and a personalization design maker in LVMH. While working. Pegah has completed an AA in Digital Media Design. She is a transfer student to San Jose state University. Her next step is to accomplish the goal of receiving Bachelor's Degree, and possibly of working as a UX/UI or a 3D motion designer..
