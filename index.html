Pegah Taheri

I am originally from Tehran/Iran. I was born and raised in Iran and moved to Turkey when I was 18. During my education I have always been drawn to the Arts. I would consider myself a creative person and would love to make artwork. So, as I got older I began to take art classes were I began learning about drawing, and design. I grew my passion in performing art in Ballet industry. I was a dancer/choreographer in San Francisco Ballet from 2016-2019. Then I joined the design/sale team in luxury fashion industry, Louis Vuitton. I currently am a client expert and a personalization design maker in LVMH. While working. I have completed an AA in Digital Media Design and then I transferred to San Jose state University. My next step is to accomplish the goal of receiving Bachelor's Degree, and possibly of working as a UX/UI or a 3D motion designer. I am also very passionate about working in the movie industry such as movie editor or as a special effect artist. I have lived in 3 different countries and speak 4 different languages. My diverse background and exposure to different cultures have shaped me into a problem solver, a facilitator, and a good prototyper. I love to interact with different people in design industry and gain insight from them. . .
